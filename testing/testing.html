Tests

While using the TuMi Mundo method, you can do a couple of different tests to measure your childâ€™s progress. You will be able to review all your results on the statistics page.

Privacy statement

The results of these tests will be shared with the researchers and universities. You can change your preferences in the settings at any given time and moment.

6-12 months

Attention span test

10-15 minutes

How it works:

The reason to do this test is to measure how long the attention span is for the learned language(s). Before you start the test there will be a few short steps you will have to take before you can start the test. This will be explained to you through a few pictures.

Word recognition

5-10 minutes

How it works:

This test is provided through the University of Leiden. The results of these tests will be shared with the researchers directly. This is optional and not part of the method or the lessons. You will still receive results after the test. This is only for research purposes. How many of the words heard during the stories stay around?
